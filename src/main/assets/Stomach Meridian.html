(ST) Stomach Meridian - Point Locations Chart

The (ST) Stomach Meridian has 45 points:


Nourishment - Physical and Emotional
1. The stomach meridian is a yang meridian and is paired with the Spleen yin meridian.
2. It functions with the Spleen Meridian in the assimilation of Qi from food through digestion and absorption.

The Stomach Meridian Route:
1. The stomach meridian starts at ST1, between the lower eyelid and the eye socket, and runs down the face and loops up to the forehead. 2. At ST8, it backtracks down across the shoulder, down the ribs, along the stomach, and then down the leg ending at ST45, the outside edge of the second toe.

Element:
Earth element with strong nurturing qualities.

Physical Imbalances:
Digestive and stomach problems - abdominal pain, distension, edema, vomiting, sore throat, facial paralysis, upper gum toothache, nose bleeding.

Emotional Imbalances:
Anxiety, worry, skepticism, poor confidence, feelings of suspicion or mistrust.

When Balanced:
Fairness, openness and caring.

Peak Hours:
7-9 am

Foods:
Root Vegetables such as carrots, parsnip, turnips, and all the other types of food that come from the earth.
