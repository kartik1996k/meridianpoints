(GB) Gall Bladder Meridian - Point Locations Chart

The (GB) Gall Bladder Meridian has 44 points:

For Courage and Initiative
1. The Gallbladder Meridian is a yang meridian and is paired with the Liver Meridian.
2. This Meridian's Qi (energy flow) is responsible, among other, for decision making and good judgments, as well as providing courage and initiative.

Gallbladder Meridian Route
1. This meridian begins just outside the outer corner of the eye, turns down towards the ear and then up to the forehead just within the hair line, then descends behind the ear to the corner of the skull.
2. It then returns to the forehead above the center of the eye and moves down the head to the bottom of the skull.
3. It continues down the neck to the shoulder, descends the side of the body along the ribs, to the waist and pelvic. It continues down the outside of the leg, to the ankle, ending on the outside of the 4th toe.


Gallbladder Meridian Element:
Wood

Physical Imbalances:
insomnia - waking up suddenly, very early in the morning and not being able to fall asleep again, tendons, tears, nails, eye diseases, glaucoma, and night blindness, stiff neck, ringing in the ears, dizziness

Emotional Imbalances:
timidity, indecision, easily discouraged... and resentment.

When Balanced:
courage and initiative, decision making and good judgment, sound sleep, healthy expression and processes of creativity, planning, brainstorming, analysis, and decision-making

Peak Hours:
11pm - 1am

Gallbladder Meridian Foods:
'Woody foods' - leafy greens, grains... A great habit to take on, is having a green smoothie (leafy greens + other veggies - juiced or blended) a day! Works wonders!
